Band Biography

DAWN OF OUROBOROS is a blackened progressive metal project that is making waves across the globe with a unique blend of death, post, and atmospheric sub-genres built upon an intoxicating European Black Metal influence . Founded and helmed by guitarist Tony Thomas (BOTANIST, CAILLEACH CALLING) and vocalist Chelsea Murphy (CAILLEACH CALLING, Session Vocalist) DAWN OF OUROBOROS took shape over the course of the last several years. They released their debut album The Art of Morphology to international critical acclaim in 2020. The album was praised for its emotional impact and originality. In Fall of 2021 they set out on their first tour as a band with the melodic extreme metal band VINTERSEA. Following the success of this tour, the band was quickly signed to long standing heavy metal label Prosthetic Records. Their second album, and first release via Prosthetic Records, Velvet Incandescence was released in Spring of 2023. The album expanded on the complex textures of their debut instrumentally, while vocalist Chelsea Murphy expanded her range as both an extreme vocalist and singer. The album painted a detailed visual and sonic landscape of color and emotion. Velvet Incandescence led the band to earning their first national tour supporting long-standing progressive metal bands FALLUJAH, and PERSEFONE in the summer of 2024. On this tour, the band earned praise from their peers, and audiences across North America. Carrying a strong momentum into 2025, the band released their third album Bioluminescence in March of 2025. Bioluminescence demonstrated that the band has fully established their sound a modern metal act to be reckoned with. On this album, they explored more progressive elements by incorporating odd time signatures, more complex harmonic elements, and an ever-increasing display of vocal diversity. This album earned the band their greatest amount of international press to date. With features in long-standing metal magazines such as Decibel, and Kerrang! Following the success of a headline west coast tour with WOLF KING, the band was offered a support tour with sludge metal legends BARONESS. In the second half of 2025, the band aims to continue touring, and to start working on the next chapter of their musical journey.
